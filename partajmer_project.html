Website for For people who want to travel South Africa

Project name: Travel Sa

Role: UI/UX, Research

Timeline: March - October 2022/p>

how might we solve

Offering services or finding the right person for their specific needs

Hypothesis

Making travelling around South Africa easy.

All vacation spots must be listed

Create network of thrustworthy customers

Transperently interact with tourists

Connect with agencies that offer accomadation to clients

Why?

People use different tools to find accomadation or advertise their services. They are often struggling to find the right sources and they are most likely to relly on recommendations.

business opportunities

This web platform would be a perfect tool for people to easily create their network of customers. Bigger community and participation of different companies/ agencies could add specific bussines value and again make travelling around South Africa safe.

Who would consider travel Za?

Most of the youth in South Africa go on vacations during holidays and festive season

I conducted conducted research based on that premisis.

Final Web Design

Scrolling Through

Step 1:
From the Home Page new users can easily get all the info about Travel Za and browse the Services offered: by keywords, location and/or category.

Step 2:
Users can view the list of available accomadation/services and refine the results by their popularity and date. Short details regarding date, time, price and description are shown on the list. Users can accsess specific services and book accomadation after the Log In or Registration process.
